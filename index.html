About us:
Our research interests focus on geometric machine learning, geometric optimization, and time series analysis. The applications include multi-sensor fusion for intelligent car, robotic vision, images, molecules and point clouds modeling, synthesis, recognition and semantics.
The laboratory currently has 2 doctors, 4 doctoral students, and 11 master students. We are recruiting students and welcome to cooperate with us.
